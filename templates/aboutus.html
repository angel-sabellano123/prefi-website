About Us

Welcome to Purely Handmade — your home for warm, heartfelt, and lovingly crafted crochet creations. We believe that every loop, stitch, and pattern tells a story of patience, creativity, and care.

Founded by Angel Sabellano, Purely Handmade is a small passion project turned into a space where people can find joy in uniquely handmade items. From soft toys and scarves to baskets and blankets, each product is made with high-quality yarn and a lot of love.

Our mission is to support local artistry, bring comfort through crafts, and share the warmth of handmade with every customer. Whether you're buying for yourself or a gift for someone special, we hope our creations bring a smile to your heart.
